Mimics Page

 A directory of mimics and their associated weapon, attacks, chips, and affinity.
Mimics are the player's representation of the characters found in Tower of Fantasy. They come with their associated weapon and have a passive effect that can be enabled through the simulacrum. Their associated chips are also listed on the, but must be obtained separately.

Chips Page

 Includes the chips that are not associated with a mimic.
Weapon attachments that provide stat boosts and special effects. There are 4 slots on each weapon; Emotion, Mind, Belief, and Memory. Each of these slots gives slightly different passive stats.

Gadgets Page

 A directory of gadgets and their effects.
Gadgets are tools that aid the player in exploration or combat.

Food Page

 A list of dishes and their recipes.
Food can provide buffs, recover HP, and recover hunger.

Mounts Page

 A list of mounts and methods for obtaining their parts.
Currently all mounts move at the same speed, so getting new ones is strictly a cosmetic flex. Multiple mounts can unlock achievements and you will gain some black crystal from leveling them up--leveling up multiple low level mounts is more cost-effective than one high level one.
